How the Predictive Model Works

CartCast forecasts retail grocery prices 4–8 weeks ahead using commodity futures and farm price data, applying the USDA ERS price transmission lag methodology.

1Commodity futures & farm prices fetched daily from FRED and USDA NASS (wheat, cattle, hogs, dairy, coffee, corn)
2Each product is assigned a transmission elasticity (0–1.0) — how much a commodity price change flows through to retail shelves
3A lag period (2–12 weeks) is applied per category — eggs react in 2 weeks; bread takes 8 weeks after a wheat spike
4Forecast = Current Price × (1 + commodity_change × elasticity × lag_fraction)
5Confidence scored by commodity volatility, data recency, and lag length — shorter lags & stable commodities score higher
